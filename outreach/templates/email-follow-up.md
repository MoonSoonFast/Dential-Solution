# Email Templates — Follow-Up Sequence

> These templates pair with the follow-up cadence in [`../04-follow-up-sequence.md`](../04-follow-up-sequence.md). Each touch adds new value — never just "checking in."

---

## Follow-Up Touch 2 (Day 3–4) — Adding a New Insight

```
Subject: Re: [Original subject line]

Hi [First Name],

Just following up on my last note — wanted to make sure the 
[free resource] didn't get buried.

One more thing I noticed while reviewing [Practice Name]: 
[new specific observation — e.g., "your top competitor has 3x more 
Google reviews and responds to every one within 24 hours"].

That gap is usually closeable in 60–90 days with the right process.

Happy to walk you through what that looks like — no obligation.

[Your Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up Touch 4 (Day 10–11) — New Value Asset

```
Subject: Something different for [Practice Name]

Hi [First Name],

I know I've sent a couple of notes without hearing back — 
I'll keep this brief.

I put together a [new asset — e.g., "1-page local SEO checklist 
specifically for dental practices"] that I think you'll find more 
immediately actionable than what I sent before.

Here it is: [Link]

If you've got 5 minutes, it's worth a look.

[Your Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up Touch 6 (Day 20) — The Break-Up Email

```
Subject: Should I close your file, [First Name]?

Hi [First Name],

I've sent a few notes your way and haven't heard back — 
completely understand if the timing isn't right or this isn't a fit.

Before I close out, one last thing:

[Final free resource — e.g., "I put together a 90-day patient acquisition 
roadmap for single-location dental practices. Here it is: [Link]"]

If this ever becomes relevant, my door is always open.

Either way, best of luck with [Practice Name] — 
[genuine compliment, e.g., "your reviews show you have something 
special with patient experience"].

[Your Name]
[Title] | [Phone] | [LinkedIn]
```

---

## Follow-Up Touch 7 (Day 30+) — Long-Term Nurture

```
Subject: Thought this would be useful for you

Hi [First Name],

Saw this and thought of [Practice Name]: 
[Link to a genuinely useful article, study, or resource]

[1–2 sentences about why it's relevant — be specific, not generic]

No agenda — just thought you'd find it useful.

[Your Name]
```

---

## Post-Response Thank You (After Any Reply)

```
Subject: Re: [Thread subject]

Thanks for getting back to me, [First Name].

[Respond directly to what they said]

[If they want more info:] I'll send over [specific thing] by [day].

[If they want to talk:] Are you free [Day 1] at [Time] or [Day 2] at [Time]?

[Your Name]
```

---

## After Discovery Call — Thank You Email

```
Subject: Great talking with you, [First Name]

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for taking the time today — I really enjoyed learning more 
about [Practice Name] and what you're building.

A few things I'm taking away from our conversation:
• [Pain 1 they mentioned]
• [Goal they shared]
• [Something specific that stood out]

As promised, I'll have a tailored proposal to you by [Date].

In the meantime, here's [one more free resource relevant to what they shared].

Looking forward to [next step].

[Your Name]
[Title] | [Phone] | [Calendar Link]
```
