# Email Templates — Cold Outreach

> Copy-paste ready templates. Always customize the `[bracketed]` sections before sending.

---

## Template 1: Website Gap Observation

**Best for:** Practices with weak or outdated websites

```
Subject: Quick win I spotted for [Practice Name]

Hi [First Name],

I was looking at [Practice Name]'s website and noticed it doesn't have 
an online booking option — you might be losing 30–40% of new patient 
inquiries that come in after hours.

I put together a quick checklist of the 5 highest-impact website fixes 
for dental practices — attached, no strings attached.

Worth a 2-minute read. Happy to answer any questions if it sparks ideas.

[Your Name]
[Title] | [Phone]
```

## Subject Line Bank

Use these tested subject lines when A/B testing your outreach:

**Value-led:**
- `Free checklist for [Practice Name]`
- `Something I made for you, [First Name]`
- `I noticed something about [Practice Name]`
- `Quick win for [Practice Name] — free`

**Curiosity-led:**
- `[Practice Name] is losing patients after hours`
- `Your competitors are doing this — you're not`
- `Something I spotted on your Google profile`

**Direct:**
- `Free 15-min audit for [Practice Name]`
- `15 minutes that could change [Practice Name]'s patient flow`
