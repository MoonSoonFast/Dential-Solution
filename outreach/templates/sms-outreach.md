# SMS Outreach Templates

> SMS has the highest open rate of any channel (90%+ within 3 minutes). Use it sparingly — only when you have a genuine reason to text, and always lead with value.

---

## ⚠️ SMS Best Practices

- Only use SMS if you have a genuine professional contact (direct number from a business card, directory, or mutual connection)
- Keep it to 160 characters ideally (one SMS)
- Always identify yourself
- Never spam — one SMS without a response means stop texting
- Respect opt-out requests immediately

---

## Template 1: After Leaving a Voicemail

```
Hi [First Name], [Your Name] here — just left you a voicemail. 
I have a free [resource] for [Practice Name] I think you'll find 
useful. Happy to send it over: [Link]. No call needed.
```

---

## Template 2: Warm Introduction (Mutual Connection)

```
Hi [First Name] — [Mutual Name] suggested I reach out. 
I have something free for [Practice Name]: [one-line description]. 
Worth a look: [Link]. Happy to answer questions!
```

---

## Template 3: Following Up on Email

```
Hi [First Name], [Your Name] — sent you an email about [Practice Name] 
last week. Happy to send the [free resource] via text instead? 
Just reply with a Y and I'll send it over.
```

---

## Template 4: After They Express Interest

```
Hi [First Name]! Great connecting. Here's the [free resource] 
I mentioned: [Link]. Let me know if you have questions — 
happy to hop on a quick call: [Calendar Link]
```

---

## Template 5: Meeting Confirmation

```
Hi [First Name], confirming our call tomorrow at [Time] — 
[Video/Phone link]. Looking forward to it! – [Your Name]
```

---

## When NOT to Use SMS

- ❌ As a first-ever touch with zero prior contact
- ❌ Multiple times without response
- ❌ After they've asked you to stop
- ❌ On weekends or before 8am / after 8pm (local time)
- ❌ For long messages (use email instead)
