# Step 4 — Follow-Up Sequence: More Value at Every Touch

> Most deals are closed on the 5th–8th follow-up. Each touch should add more value — not just "checking in."

---

## 📅 Recommended Follow-Up Cadence

| Touch | Day | Channel | Action |
|-------|-----|---------|--------|
| **Touch 1** | Day 0 | Email | First contact + free asset (Step 3) |
| **Touch 2** | Day 3–4 | Email | Reply to first email + new value insight |
| **Touch 3** | Day 7 | LinkedIn | Connect or engage with their content |
| **Touch 4** | Day 10–11 | Email | Different value asset or case study |
| **Touch 5** | Day 15 | Phone | Brief call attempt with voicemail |
| **Touch 6** | Day 20 | Email | Final value offer + break-up message |
| **Touch 7** | Day 30 | Email/LinkedIn | "Hail Mary" — long-term nurture |

**Total active sequence:** ~30 days  
**After 30 days:** Move to monthly nurture (newsletter, helpful articles, congratulatory notes on milestones)

---

## 📝 Follow-Up Message Templates

### Touch 2 — Email Reply (Day 3–4)

```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

[First name],

Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried — I included [free asset] 
in my last message and I think it could be genuinely useful for [specific reason].

One thing I noticed while putting it together: [new personalized insight]. 
[One sentence explaining why that matters to their practice.]

Happy to answer any questions — no obligation to do anything else.

[Name]
```

---

### Touch 3 — LinkedIn Message (Day 7)

```

wanted to connect here in case that's easier.

Either way, feel free to grab the [resource] — link: [URL]

Best,
[Name]
```

### Touch 4 — New Value Email (Day 10–11)

```
Subject: [Something new and specific to their practice]

[First name],

I've been thinking about [specific thing you noticed about their practice] 
and put together [new free asset or insight].

[2–3 sentence description of the value — what it is, why it matters, what they can do with it]

[Link or attachment]

Worth a 5-minute look. Let me know what you think.

[Name]
```

```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Either way, best of luck with [Practice Name] — you're clearly doing 
[something positive you genuinely noticed].

[Name]
```

### Touch 7 — Long-Term Nurture (Day 30+)

```
Subject: [Timely article or insight relevant to their practice]

[First name],

Saw this and thought of [Practice Name]: [link to genuinely useful article or resource]

[1–2 sentences about why it's relevant to them specifically]

No agenda — just thought you'd find it useful.

[Name]
```

---

## 🔄 Handling Common Responses

### "Not interested"
```
Totally understand. Out of curiosity — is the timing off, or is it not 
the right fit right now? Either answer helps me. No pressure either way.
```

### "Send me more information"
Send a one-page overview + a relevant case study. Don't send a generic brochure.

### "Call me" / "Let's talk"
Go directly to → [`05-discovery-call.md`](05-discovery-call.md)

### No response after 6 touches
Move to monthly nurture. Re-engage in 60–90 days with a fresh value asset.
