# Step 3 — First Contact: Lead with Value

> Your first message should make the recipient's day better — even if they never work with you. That's the value-first difference.

---

## 🧠 The Value-First First Contact Framework

**Wrong approach:** "Hi, I help dental practices grow. Can I get 15 minutes of your time?"  
**Right approach:** "Hi, I noticed [specific problem]. I put together something that solves exactly that — here it is, no strings attached."

The formula:
```
[Personalized observation] + [Free valuable asset] + [Soft ask]
```

---

## 📧 Channel Selection Guide

Choose your first-touch channel based on what you know about the prospect:

| Channel | Best When | Response Rate |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **Email** | You have a direct or practice email | 15–25% |
| **LinkedIn DM** | They're active on LinkedIn | 20–35% |
| **Phone call** | You want immediate conversation | 5–15% connect rate |
| **SMS** | You have a mobile number and warm intro | 30–45% |
| **Physical mail** | High-value targets, standing out | Varies |

**Recommendation:** Email is the default. Use LinkedIn for owner/doctors. Use phone for follow-up after no email response.

---

## 📬 First Contact — Email Strategy

### Subject Lines That Work

Lead with value in the subject line:

- `Free checklist for [Practice Name]'s online reviews`
- `Quick win I spotted for [Practice Name]`
- `Your free patient acquisition guide (no catch)`
- `I made something for [Practice Name]`
- `[Specific observation] — here's the fix`

### Email Structure (Under 150 Words)

```
[First name],

[1 sentence: specific personalized observation about their practice]

[1–2 sentences: free value you're offering and why it's relevant to them]

[Link or attachment to the free resource]

[1 sentence: soft ask — reply, or no pressure if not relevant]

[Your name]
[Your title]
[Phone / LinkedIn]
```

## 💼 LinkedIn First Contact Strategy

LinkedIn works especially well for practice owners and dentists who are active on the platform.

**Step 1: Follow them first** (3–5 days before messaging)  
**Step 2: Engage with their content** (like or comment on a recent post)  
**Step 3: Send connection request** with a personalized note  
**Step 4: After they accept,** send the value-first message

## 📞 Phone Outreach Strategy

Use phone as a supplement, not a primary channel. The goal of a cold call is to get permission to send your value asset, not to pitch.

**Script goal:** "I'd love to send you something useful — what's the best email?"

## 🎁 What to Offer as Free Value (First Touch)

The free asset you offer should be:
- **Immediately useful** — they can act on it today
- **Specific to their situation** — not generic
- **Low time investment to consume** — 1–5 minute read
- **Demonstrating your expertise** — without being a sales pitch

**Best first-touch assets:**
| Asset | Best For |
|-------|---------|
| Practice website audit (1-page PDF) | Practices with weak websites |
| Google review response template pack | Practices with unanswered reviews |
| "5 Quick Wins" checklist for their specific gap | Any practice with clear problems |
| Patient acquisition guide | Growth-focused practices |
| Local SEO checklist | Practices ranking poorly on Google |
