# Step 5 — The Discovery Call

> The discovery call is where you listen, not pitch. Your job is to understand their world so thoroughly that your solution becomes obvious.

---

## 🎯 Discovery Call Goals

1. **Understand** their current situation, challenges, and goals
2. **Qualify** them as the right fit (for both sides)
3. **Identify** the specific pain your solution addresses
4. **Build trust** through curiosity and relevance
5. **Set up** the proposal or next step naturally

---

## 📋 Pre-Call Preparation (10 minutes)

Before the call, review:
- [ ] Your research notes from Step 2
- [ ] Any emails or messages they replied to
- [ ] Their website, reviews, and social media (fresh look)
- [ ] Your proposed value asset to deliver on the call (ready to screen share)

---

## 📞 Call Structure (30–45 minutes)

### Opening (2–3 minutes)
- Thank them for their time
- Set expectations for the call
- Confirm the time available

```
"Thanks so much for making time, [Name]. I have us scheduled for 30 minutes — 
does that still work? 

Quick agenda: I'd love to hear a bit about [Practice Name] and what's 
top of mind for you right now, and I can share a few ideas based on what 
I've observed. Sound good?"
```

---

### Discovery Questions (15–20 minutes)

#### Practice Foundations
- "Tell me about the practice — how long have you been open, and how has it grown?"
- "How many dentists / hygienists are on the team right now?"
- "What types of patients do you primarily serve?"

#### Current Challenges
- "What's your biggest challenge when it comes to growing the practice right now?"
- "If you could fix one thing tomorrow, what would it be?"
- "How are most of your new patients finding you today?"

#### Goals and Vision
- "What does success look like for you 12 months from now?"
- "Are you focused more on adding new patients, or maximizing the value of existing patients?"
- "Is expansion or adding locations something you're thinking about?"

#### Current Situation (Pain Awareness)
- "How are you currently handling [specific gap you identified in research]?"
- "What have you already tried to improve [pain area]?"
- "How is that working for you?"

#### Decision Process
- "If we found the right solution today, what would the process look like to get it started?"
- "Is there anyone else involved in decisions like this?"
- "What would need to be true for you to feel confident moving forward?"

---

### Present Tailored Value (10 minutes)

After listening, share the specific free asset or audit you prepared:

```
"Based on what you've shared, I actually have something built specifically 
for this situation. Can I share my screen for 2 minutes?"
```

Walk them through:
1. What you found (specific to their practice)
2. What it's costing them (quantify where possible)
3. What the fix looks like (your solution)

See the free audit framework → [`../value-first/free-audit-offer.md`](../value-first/free-audit-offer.md)

---

### Close the Call (5 minutes)

Don't close the deal on the discovery call. Close the **next step**.

```
"Based on everything you've shared, I think there's a real opportunity here. 
What I'd like to do is put together a specific proposal for [Practice Name] 
that addresses [top pain] and [second pain]. Can we schedule a 30-minute 
follow-up in the next week or so to walk through it?"
```
