# Voicemail Scripts

> The goal of a voicemail is to get a callback — not to pitch. Keep it under 25 seconds. Speak slowly and repeat your number twice.

---

## Voicemail Script 1: First Contact

```
"Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name] calling about [Practice Name]. 
I have a free [resource — e.g., 'practice audit'] I'd love to send your way — 
there's no catch, it's just something I put together that I think 
you'll find genuinely useful. Give me a call back at [Phone Number] 
— that's [Phone Number] — or feel free to just shoot me a text. 
Either way, hope you have a great day."
```

**Duration:** ~18 seconds

---

## Voicemail Script 2: Follow-Up After Email

```
"Hi [First Name], [Your Name] again — I sent you an email last week 
with a free [resource] for [Practice Name] and wanted to make sure 
it didn't get lost in the inbox. If it's easier, I can just text it 
to you directly. Reach me at [Phone Number] — that's [Phone Number]. 
Have a great one."
```

**Duration:** ~18 seconds

---

## Voicemail Script 3: Value-First With Urgency

```
"Hi [First Name], [Your Name] here. Quick one — I did a short audit 
of [Practice Name]'s online presence and found something specific 
I'd love to share. Takes 10 minutes and there's no pitch. 
[Phone Number] — that's [Phone Number]. Happy to follow up by email too."
```

**Duration:** ~17 seconds

---

## Voicemail Script 4: Final Attempt (Break-Up)

```
"Hi [First Name], [Your Name] — this'll be my last message. 
I have one more free resource for [Practice Name] I'd like to send you. 
If you're interested, just text me 'yes' at [Phone Number] 
and I'll send it right over. No pressure at all. Best of luck."
```

**Duration:** ~18 seconds

---

## ✅ Voicemail Best Practices

- 🗣️ **Slow down** — speak 20% slower than your normal pace
- 📞 **Repeat your number twice** — they're often driving
- ⏱️ **Keep it under 25 seconds** — longer voicemails are skipped
- 🎁 **Lead with value** — mention the free resource immediately
- 🤝 **No pitch** — don't mention pricing, packages, or closing
- ✉️ **Follow up by email** — always send a follow-up email right after leaving a voicemail
