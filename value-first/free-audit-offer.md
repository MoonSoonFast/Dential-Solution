# How to Deliver a Free Practice Audit

> The free audit is the single most powerful value-first tool in this playbook. Done right, it earns more trust in 15 minutes than any email could in 10 touches.

---

## What the Free Audit Is

A personalized review of a dental practice's online presence, delivered as a gift — with no strings attached. You show them what you found, explain what it means, and offer a path forward.

**What it covers:**
1. Google Business Profile (completeness, ratings, response rate)
2. Website (speed, UX, conversion elements)
3. Online booking capability
4. Social media presence and engagement
5. Local SEO visibility
6. Competitor snapshot

**Time investment (your side):** 20–30 minutes of research  
**Delivery format:** 15-minute video call OR pre-recorded Loom video

---

## When to Offer the Audit

| Situation | When to offer |
|-----------|--------------|
| High-value prospect | As a first-touch outreach offer |
| Prospect who replied to email | After first or second follow-up |
| Post-discovery call | As a "leave-behind" to add more value |
| Prospects who went quiet | As a re-engagement tool |

## How to Prepare the Audit (30 Minutes)

### Step 1: Google Business Profile Review (5 min)
- [ ] Star rating and total reviews
- [ ] Response rate (especially negative)
- [ ] Last post date
- [ ] Profile completeness score (photos, hours, services listed)
- [ ] Keyword optimization in the business description

**Tools:** Google Maps, BrightLocal (free trial)

---

### Step 2: Website Review (10 min)
- [ ] Page speed: run through [PageSpeed Insights](https://pagespeed.web.dev)
- [ ] Mobile responsiveness: use [Google Mobile-Friendly Test](https://search.google.com/test/mobile-friendly)
- [ ] Homepage CTA: is there a prominent "Book Now" or "Call" button above the fold?
- [ ] Online booking: does it exist? Is it easy to find?
- [ ] Social proof: testimonials, reviews, before/after on site?
- [ ] Trust signals: SSL, HIPAA notice, staff photos?
- [ ] Blog/content: any? When was the last post?

**Screenshot 3–5 key findings with annotations**

---

### Step 3: Local SEO Check (5 min)
- [ ] How do they rank for "[service] [city]" on Google?
- [ ] Is their NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent across directories?
- [ ] Are they listed on: Yelp, Healthgrades, Zocdoc, WebMD?

**Tools:** [BrightLocal](https://www.brightlocal.com), [Moz Local](https://moz.com/local)

---

### Step 4: Social Media Check (5 min)
- [ ] Facebook: followers, last post, engagement rate
- [ ] Instagram: followers, last post, engagement
- [ ] Content quality: educational, promotional, personal mix?

---

### Step 5: Competitive Snapshot (5 min)
- [ ] Who are the top 3 competitors on Google Maps?
- [ ] How does their star rating compare?
- [ ] What do competitors do better?

## Delivering the Audit Live (Video Call)

### Opening (1 minute)
> "Thanks for making time. I spent about 20–30 minutes reviewing [Practice Name] across a few areas — I'll walk you through what I found. Feel free to stop me with questions."

### Walkthrough (8–10 minutes)

For each finding, use this structure:
1. **What I found:** Show the specific observation (share screen)
2. **Why it matters:** Quantify the impact where possible
3. **Quick win:** Suggest 1 action they could take this week

Example:
> "I ran your site through PageSpeed Insights — it loaded in 6.2 seconds on mobile. The benchmark for patient retention is under 3 seconds, and Google actually ranks faster sites higher. The quick fix is usually image compression, which can be done in an afternoon."

### Closing (2 minutes)
> "Those are my top findings. Overall, [Practice Name] has a strong foundation in [area], and the biggest opportunity is [top finding]. 
>
> Happy to go deeper on any of this — and if you're interested in exploring how to tackle these systematically, I'm happy to put together some specific recommendations. But there's no pressure — this audit is yours to keep either way."

## Delivering the Audit Asynchronously (Loom Video)

If they're hard to schedule or you want to send something before a call:

**Loom video structure:**
1. Intro: "Hi [Name], [Your Name] here — I did a quick audit of [Practice Name] and want to share what I found."
2. Screen share: Walk through each finding
3. Close: "Feel free to reach out with questions — and if you'd like to tackle any of this, I'd love to chat."

**Target length:** 4–7 minutes  
**Tool:** [Loom.com](https://www.loom.com) (free tier works)

## After the Audit

- [ ] Send a written summary (email) with top 3 findings
- [ ] Include a 1-page PDF screenshot of key issues
- [ ] Follow up in 3 days: "Did the audit spark any questions?"
- [ ] Log in CRM: what they reacted to most strongly

The audit findings become the foundation for your proposal (Step 6).
