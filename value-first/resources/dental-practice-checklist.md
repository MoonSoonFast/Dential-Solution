# Dental Practice Self-Assessment Checklist

> A free resource for dental practices to assess their current marketing and online presence. Use as a value-first offer in outreach.

---

## 📋 How to Use This Checklist

Score each item:
- ✅ **Done** — in place and working well
- ⚠️ **Partial** — exists but needs improvement
- ❌ **Missing** — not in place

At the end, count your score and find your category.
