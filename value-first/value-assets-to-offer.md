# 12 Value Assets to Offer Dental Practices

> These are things you can offer for free during outreach to build trust and demonstrate expertise before asking for anything in return.

---

## How to Choose the Right Asset

Match the asset to what you observed in your research (Step 2). The more specific the asset is to their situation, the more likely they are to engage.

| If you observed... | Offer this asset |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| Unanswered negative reviews | Review Response Template Pack (#1) |
| Low Google star rating | Online Reputation Guide (#2) |
| Weak or outdated website | Website Quick-Win Audit (#3) |
| No online booking | Patient Booking Optimization Guide (#4) |
| Inactive social media | 30-Day Content Calendar (#5) |
| Low local search ranking | Local SEO Checklist (#6) |
| No blog or content | Patient Education Content Pack (#7) |
| High-growth signals | New Patient Acquisition Roadmap (#8) |
| DSO or multi-location | Practice Growth Playbook (#9) |
| High competitor ratings | Competitive Analysis Snapshot (#10) |
| Team/hiring activity | Team Culture & Retention Guide (#11) |
| Any practice | Free 15-Minute Practice Audit (#12) |

## The 12 Assets

### 1. 📝 Review Response Template Pack
**What it is:** 10 word-for-word responses for the most common dental review scenarios (positive, neutral, negative).  
**Why it works:** Unanswered reviews are a massive missed opportunity. This solves an immediate, visible pain.  
**Format:** PDF or Google Doc

**Talking point:**
> "Responding to reviews — including bad ones — is one of the highest-ROI things a practice can do. Most practices don't do it because they don't know what to say. This removes that barrier."

---

### 2. ⭐ Online Reputation Improvement Guide
**What it is:** Step-by-step guide to building a consistent 5-star presence on Google, Healthgrades, and Zocdoc.  
**Why it works:** New patients almost always check reviews before calling. A better reputation = more calls.  
**Format:** PDF guide (1–3 pages)

---

### 3. 🌐 Website Quick-Win Audit
**What it is:** A personalized 1-page report on the 3–5 highest-impact improvements for their specific website.  
**Why it works:** It's personalized, visual, and immediately actionable.  
**Format:** 1-page PDF with screenshots and annotations

**Talking point:**
> "I spent 20 minutes on your website and flagged the 3 things that are likely causing you to lose new patients before they ever call. Here it is."

See full audit framework → [`../solution/audit/audit-framework.md`](../solution/audit/audit-framework.md)

---

### 4. 📅 Patient Booking Optimization Guide
**What it is:** A guide to reducing friction in the online booking experience.  
**Why it works:** 70% of patients prefer online booking. Practices without it lose appointments daily.  
**Format:** 1-page PDF or email

---

### 5. 📱 30-Day Dental Content Calendar
**What it is:** A month of pre-planned social media content ideas (with captions) designed for dental practices.  
**Why it works:** Social media inactivity is common. This removes the biggest barrier: "what to post."  
**Format:** Google Sheets or Notion template

---

### 6. 🔍 Local SEO Checklist for Dental Practices
**What it is:** A checklist of everything a dental practice should have in place to rank well on Google Maps and local search.  
**Why it works:** Most practices have no idea how local SEO works. The checklist makes it simple and actionable.  
**Format:** PDF checklist

---

### 7. 📰 Patient Education Content Pack
**What it is:** 5–10 pre-written patient-facing social posts or blog articles (e.g., "5 Signs You Need a Night Guard").  
**Why it works:** Educational content builds authority and keeps the practice top-of-mind with existing patients.  
**Format:** Google Doc with ready-to-post content

---

### 8. 🚀 New Patient Acquisition Roadmap
**What it is:** A 90-day framework for systematically growing a practice's new patient count.  
**Why it works:** Growth-focused practices love structured plans. This shows you understand their goal.  
**Format:** 1-page visual PDF or slide deck

See full version → [`resources/patient-acquisition-guide.md`](resources/patient-acquisition-guide.md)

---

### 9. 📊 Practice Growth Playbook (DSO Edition)
**What it is:** A multi-location expansion framework covering operations, marketing, and patient experience.  
**Why it works:** DSOs and group practices have different needs. A tailored playbook shows you understand their world.  
**Format:** Slide deck or PDF

---

### 10. 🏆 Competitive Analysis Snapshot
**What it is:** A quick visual comparison of their practice vs. top 3 local competitors across Google ratings, website quality, and social presence.  
**Why it works:** No practice owner can ignore a visual showing them losing to a competitor.  
**Format:** 1-page PDF

---

### 11. 👥 Team Culture & Patient Experience Guide
**What it is:** A guide to staff communication, onboarding, and patient touchpoints that improve retention and reviews.  
**Why it works:** Strong team culture leads to stronger reviews and lower turnover — both critical for growth.  
**Format:** PDF or email guide

---

### 12. 🔎 Free 15-Minute Practice Audit (Live or Async)
**What it is:** A personalized walk-through of your observations on their practice — website, reviews, social, local SEO.  
**Why it works:** This is the highest-converting value asset because it's real-time, personal, and demonstrates expertise live.  
**Format:** Video call (screen share) or recorded Loom video

See full audit process → [`free-audit-offer.md`](free-audit-offer.md)

## 📦 Building Your Asset Library

You don't need all 12 from day one. Start with 3–4 that match your most common prospect profiles.

**Recommended starter kit:**
1. Review Response Template Pack (#1)
2. Website Quick-Win Audit framework (#3)
3. Local SEO Checklist (#6)
4. Free 15-Minute Practice Audit offer (#12)

These four cover the gaps you'll see in 80%+ of dental practice prospects.
