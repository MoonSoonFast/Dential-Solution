# Case Studies

> Real-world examples demonstrate your capability better than any pitch deck. Use this folder to document results you achieve with clients.

---

## Why Case Studies Matter in Outreach

Case studies are one of the most powerful value-first assets because they:

- **Prove your claims** with specific numbers
- **Build trust** by showing real outcomes, not promises
- **Reduce perceived risk** — prospects see others like them succeeding
- **Are highly shareable** — a good case study gets forwarded

---

## How to Use Case Studies in Outreach

| When to share | How to use it |
|--------------|---------------|
| Follow-up touch 4 | "Here's what we did for a similar practice in [City]" |
| Post-discovery call | Attach to thank-you email |
| Proposal section 3 | "What you can expect — based on results like these" |
| LinkedIn content | Share as a post or article |
