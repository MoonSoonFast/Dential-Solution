## What to Add Here

- `brand-colors.md` — Hex codes and usage guidelines for your brand colors
- `audit-report-template.pdf` — PDF version of the audit report
- `presentation-deck.pptx` — Slide deck for proposals
- `onboarding-welcome-deck.pdf` — Client-facing welcome materials
